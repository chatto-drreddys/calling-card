# Module 1 Reflection: The Cookie Video

## Scenario Summary

In the video, an old lady buys a pack of cookies from a vending machine. After struggling with the machine, she finally gets the cookies and sits down at the railway station. A man is sitting beside her. She opens what she believes is her cookie pack and starts eating. The man also takes a cookie, which makes her angry. He continues sharing the cookies calmly, and when only one cookie is left, he breaks it in half and gives half to her. Later, when she boards the train and looks inside her bag, she realizes that her own cookie pack was there the whole time. The cookies they had been eating actually belonged to the man.

## Observations About the Old Lady

- She was determined and persistent when dealing with the vending machine.
- She became upset quickly and reacted based on her assumptions.
- She did not pause to check the situation before judging the man.
- Her frustration may have affected how she interpreted his actions.
- In the end, she realized her mistake, which shows the importance of self-reflection.

## Observations About the Guy

- He appeared calm, patient, and respectful throughout the situation.
- He did not argue or embarrass the old lady even when she seemed angry.
- He showed kindness by continuing to share the cookies.
- Splitting the last cookie in half showed generosity and emotional maturity.
- He seemed understanding and chose peace over conflict.

## What I Would Have Done Differently

- I would pause first instead of reacting immediately.
- I would politely ask, "Excuse me, are these your cookies?" before making assumptions.
- I would check my bag or my belongings to confirm the facts.
- If I were the man, I might kindly explain the misunderstanding earlier to avoid confusion.
- If I realized I was wrong, I would apologize sincerely right away.

## Key Lessons From the Video

- We should not judge people too quickly.
- Assumptions can easily lead to misunderstanding and conflict.
- Emotional control is important, especially when we are already frustrated.
- Kindness and patience can prevent small problems from becoming bigger.
- It is important to verify facts before reacting.

## Personal Reflection

If I were in that situation, I would want to stay calm and ask questions first. This video reminds me that not every situation is what it seems. Sometimes other people are more patient and kind to us than we realize. The best response is to think first, verify the facts, and treat others with respect.
